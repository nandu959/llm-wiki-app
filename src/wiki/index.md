# LLM Wiki Index

## Overview

The [[LLM Wiki]] serves as a collaborative knowledge base designed to synthesize research notes surrounding large language models, advanced retrieval techniques, and personal knowledge management systems. It aims to consolidate core concepts, comparisons, and sourced material into an easily navigable reference point.

***

## 📚 Core Concepts (Concepts)

This section explains fundamental ideas that underpin the wiki's topics.

*   [[LLM Wiki]]: A method of structuring and linking technical documentation around language model outputs and research insights.
*   [[Memex]]: A system for capturing, connecting, and retrieving scattered knowledge notes or memories.
*   [[Personal Knowledge Management]]: General principles and methodologies for organizing personal learning and intellectual output.
*   [[Retrieval-Augmented Generation]]: (RAG) Techniques that improve LLM outputs by integrating external knowledge retrieval.
