# Source: Vannevar Bush — As We May Think

## Source Type

Essay.

## Summary

Vannevar Bush's 1945 essay discusses the challenge of making accumulated knowledge more accessible.

He imagines the Memex, a hypothetical device that would help individuals store, retrieve, and connect information through associative trails.

## Key Ideas

- Information overload is a long-standing problem.
- Human thought often works by association.
- Knowledge systems should support linked trails between ideas.
- The Memex anticipates later ideas in hypertext and personal knowledge management.

- Memex
- Personal knowledge management
