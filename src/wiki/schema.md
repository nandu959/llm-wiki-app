# LLM Wiki Schema

## Purpose

This wiki is a small research knowledge base about LLM Wiki, RAG, Memex, and personal knowledge management.

## Core Principles

- Raw source notes remain the source basis.
- Wiki pages are compiled summaries and syntheses, not replacements for original sources.
- Existing pages should be updated before creating duplicate pages.
- Preserve disagreement and uncertainty.
- Do not smooth over contradictions.
- Important claims should include a short source basis section.
- Use wikilinks for internal links, for example [[LLM Wiki]].
- Record meaningful changes in `log.md`.
- Maintenance issues should be recorded in `audit.md`.

## Page Types

### Source pages

Source pages live in `wiki/sources/`.

They summarise individual source documents.

### Concept pages

Concept pages live in `wiki/concepts/`.

They explain reusable ideas such as LLM Wiki, RAG, Memex, or personal knowledge management.

### Comparison pages

Comparison pages live in `wiki/comparisons/`.

They compare related ideas, systems, or patterns.

### People pages

People pages live in `wiki/people/`.

They briefly describe relevant people and their connection to the topic.

## Writing Style

- Use clear headings.
- Prefer precise prose over hype.
- Avoid unsupported claims.
- Label interpretation as interpretation.
- Preserve uncertainty where sources disagree.
- Do not invent citations.
